About The Course

Interior Theory mainly deals with designing or planning the interior of a building. The one who plans and designs the interior of a building is an interior designer. Interior designers make the interior spaces more beautiful and creative by specifying the space requirements. They make use of Blueprints to work on interiors. And they must be aware of building codes and inspection regulations as well as universal accessibility standards. Interior design course or profession includes conceptual development, space planning, site inspections, programming, research, communicating with the stakeholders of a project, construction management, and execution of the design. Interior design is mainly all about how we experience spaces inside a building. The profession is creative and technical solutions used to achieve beautiful and safe built interior environments. Designs are created according to the space, type of space and the physical location of the project. The interior design process follows a systematic method, including many research, analysis and integration of knowledge into the creative process.
